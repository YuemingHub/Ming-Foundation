# Mission

## Canonical statement

> **Build the foundational standards and infrastructure for Living Intelligence.**

> **构建生命智能时代的基础标准与基础设施。**

## Meaning

Living Intelligence is intelligence that remains grounded in life, relationship, context, uncertainty, growth, and human agency.

The mission is not to make technology the authority over life. It is to make technology more capable of:

- observing without rushing to judge;
- remembering without imprisoning a person in the past;
- reasoning without claiming certainty it does not possess;
- supporting choice without replacing the chooser;
- learning without exploiting vulnerability;
- helping people, families, and communities understand themselves more truthfully.

## Mission boundary

Ming Foundation will not pursue growth, efficiency, engagement, prediction, or automation as unconditional goods. Such goals are legitimate only when they remain subordinate to life, dignity, safety, relationship, and agency.
