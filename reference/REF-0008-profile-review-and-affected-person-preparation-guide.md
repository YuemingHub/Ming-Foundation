# REF-0008 — Profile Review and Affected-Person Preparation Guide

## State distinctions

```text
Designed
≠ internally reviewed

Internally reviewed
≠ revised

Revised
≠ affected-person reviewed

Review prepared
≠ review executed

Participant evidence collected
≠ participant agreement

Profile operationally testable
≠ legally valid
≠ product conforming
≠ promoted
```

## Required evidence sequence

```text
Internal review
  ↓
Revision plan
  ↓
Safeguarded review preparation
  ↓
Profile source revision
  ↓
Internal re-review
  ↓
Affected-person and domain review execution
  ↓
Synthesis preserving disagreement
  ↓
Jurisdiction qualification
  ↓
Separate status decision
```
