## Profile decisions

The profiles introduce:

- participation levels P0 through P5;
- representative evidence levels E0 through E4;
- retention classes T0 through T5;
- deletion and backup completion states;
- service classes S0 through S3;
- resource freshness classes F0 through F3.

## Status boundary

Profile design does not:

- establish universal legal age or capacity;
- validate representative authority in a jurisdiction;
- prove retention legality;
- prove staffing or response performance;
- complete affected-person review;
- close an ambiguity;
- promote an RFC.
