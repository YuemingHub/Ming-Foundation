# GOV-TPL-0022 — Incident Disclosure and Stop Event Record


Record minimum necessary event information, stop time, immediate protection,
real-evidence detection, access restriction, deletion or preservation basis,
responsible owner, escalation, communication, review, and closure. Do not
copy the sensitive narrative into the general record.


Day 16 provides the template only. It is not evidence of a real assignment,
approval, environment, incident, or pilot decision.
