# GOV-TPL-0011 — Adolescent Profile Review Guide

## Review themes

- purpose-specific participation rather than maturity scoring;
- disagreement, refusal, privacy, and correction;
- parent, caregiver, and representative limits;
- retention and deletion transparency;
- safety labels and appeal;
- honest service and human-handoff claims.

## Prompts

1. Could these levels be used to control or punish someone?
2. Does the person know what they may approve, reject, correct, or revoke?
3. Could silence, distress, or disagreement be mistaken for incapacity?
4. What should a representative be unable to do?
5. What evidence of authority feels proportionate or invasive?
6. Can the person understand what data remains after deletion?
7. Could a safety label follow someone after it is wrong or outdated?
8. Which service wording would create false reassurance?
9. What is missing for privacy, disability, language, or accessibility?
10. Which assumption should be rejected rather than merely revised?

Do not request personal case details.
