# GOV-TPL-0014 — Privacy, Data-Rights, and Safety Review Guide

Review whether the profiles:

- collect only minimum necessary evidence;
- prevent representative evidence from becoming a general profile;
- support correction, restriction, deletion, expiry, and appeal;
- protect reporters and affected people;
- prevent temporary safety actions from becoming permanent control;
- distinguish audit evidence from active use;
- expose backup and exception states honestly;
- prevent false human-monitoring or response claims;
- preserve disagreement and false-positive evidence.
