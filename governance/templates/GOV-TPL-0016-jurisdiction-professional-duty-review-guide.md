# GOV-TPL-0016 — Jurisdiction and Professional-Duty Review Guide

Identify:

- operating jurisdiction and service context;
- age, capacity, consent, assent, and representative rules;
- safeguarding and mandatory-disclosure duties;
- professional licensing and scope limits;
- retention, deletion, legal hold, and audit requirements;
- emergency and service-response obligations;
- public resource and consumer-protection requirements;
- conflicts, uncertainty, and required qualification.

Output states:

```text
Compatible
CompatibleWithQualification
NeedsRevision
ProhibitedInContext
InsufficientEvidence
```

This guide must be completed by a qualified reviewer for the stated
jurisdiction and context.
