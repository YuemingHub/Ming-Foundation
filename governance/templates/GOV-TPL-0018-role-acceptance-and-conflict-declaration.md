# GOV-TPL-0018 — Role Acceptance and Conflict Declaration


Record role, named assignee, scope, expiry, qualification evidence, conflict
assessment, independence limits, prohibited actions, recusal route, backup,
and signed acceptance. Do not place sensitive personal details in the public
repository.


Day 16 provides the template only. It is not evidence of a real assignment,
approval, environment, incident, or pilot decision.
