# GOV-TPL-0015 — Accessibility, Language, and Communication Review Guide

Test:

- plain-language comprehension;
- screen-reader and non-visual use;
- low-literacy and cognitive-load conditions;
- supported and augmentative communication;
- neurodivergence and trauma-sensitive interaction;
- language and interpretation support;
- remote, low-bandwidth, and no-document access;
- use under stress;
- visibility of refusal, pause, help, correction, and appeal.

A profile fails accessibility review when a right exists in theory but cannot
be exercised through a usable channel.
