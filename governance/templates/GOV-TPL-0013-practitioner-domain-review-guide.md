# GOV-TPL-0013 — Practitioner and Domain Review Guide

Assess:

- definition precision;
- authority and conflict boundaries;
- risk of diagnostic, institutional, or professional overreach;
- safeguarding and operator-as-risk scenarios;
- retention and evidence duties;
- service availability and failed handoff;
- accessibility and implementation feasibility;
- missing counterexamples.

For every finding, record:

```text
profile
section
finding
affected people
harm or failure mode
evidence or experience basis
recommended change
minority or competing view
required review class
```

Do not treat professional status as authority to override affected-person
evidence.
