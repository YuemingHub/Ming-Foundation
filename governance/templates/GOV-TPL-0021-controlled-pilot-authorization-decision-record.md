# GOV-TPL-0021 — Controlled Pilot Authorization Decision Record


Record pilot class, exact scope, data class, participants, systems, allowed
and prohibited actions, evidence boundary, stop and revocation triggers,
authorizer, independent reviewer, expiry, and decision.


Day 16 provides the template only. It is not evidence of a real assignment,
approval, environment, incident, or pilot decision.
