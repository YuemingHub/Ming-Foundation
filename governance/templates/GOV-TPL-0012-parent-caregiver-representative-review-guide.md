# GOV-TPL-0012 — Parent, Caregiver, and Representative Review Guide

## Prompts

1. Are care, support, emergency contact, and legal authority clearly
   distinguished?
2. Which routine actions should not require formal evidence?
3. Which high-impact actions require stronger evidence?
4. Could verification exclude families without documents, technology, or
   institutional access?
5. How should the child or represented person challenge a decision?
6. When should another reviewer be required?
7. Are retention, backup, and exception states understandable?
8. Would the service classes create realistic expectations?
9. Where could a representative's interest conflict with the subject?
10. What would make the profiles less controlling and more supportive?

Do not submit identifiable family cases through this guide.
