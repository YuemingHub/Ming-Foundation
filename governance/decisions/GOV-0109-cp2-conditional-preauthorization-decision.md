# GOV-0109 — CP2 Conditional Pre-Authorization Decision

## Decision

The Architecture Board conditionally approves the preparatory work required
to seek CP2 activation.

```text
Decision:
ConditionallyApprovedNotEffective

Actual CP2 authorization:
Blocked

CP2 execution:
NotExecuted
```

## Preparatory work allowed

- restricted nomination;
- voluntary acceptance and verification;
- applicable protocol sign-off;
- minimum environment deployment and verification;
- preparation of a separate CP2 activation decision.

## Work not allowed

- execution of CP2;
- CP3 or affected-person activity;
- affected-person recruitment;
- participant or case evidence;
- live product effects;
- product-conformance claims.

Conditional pre-authorization has no effect until every condition precedent
and a separate activation decision are complete.
