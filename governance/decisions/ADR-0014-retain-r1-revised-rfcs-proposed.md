# ADR-0014 — Retain R1 Revised RFCs at Proposed Pending Round 2 Review

## Decision

The following revised source documents remain `Proposed`:

```text
RFC-0001  0.2.0-draft.1
RFC-0002  0.2.0-draft.1
RFC-0003  0.2.0-draft.1
```

## Reasons

- twelve ambiguity responses are implemented in draft source but have not
  completed Internal Architecture Review Round 2;
- affected-person, domain, legal, privacy, safety, and external review remain
  incomplete where applicable;
- acceptance tests were updated as specifications but were not executed;
- dissent remains open;
- RFC-0004 and RFC-0005 have not yet received R2 source revisions.

“Implemented in draft” is not “resolved,” “reviewed,” or “Candidate.”
