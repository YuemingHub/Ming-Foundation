# ADR-0023 — Authorize CP0 and CP1 Controlled Pilot Only

## Authorized

- CP0 document tabletop walkthrough;
- CP1 synthetic-data repository rehearsal.

## Not authorized

- staff rehearsal presented as an approved review operation;
- participant recruitment;
- child, adolescent, parent, caregiver, practitioner, client, or public
  sessions;
- live or identifiable evidence;
- production-system effects;
- safety, service, legal, professional, or conformance claims.

## Revocation

Authorization ends upon material scope change, validation failure, discovery
of real evidence, or conflict with an Accepted decision.

## Day 17 confirmation

CP0 and CP1 remain the only authorized pilot classes.

CP2 and CP3 remain blocked under ADR-0025.
