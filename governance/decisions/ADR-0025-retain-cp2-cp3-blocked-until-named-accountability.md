# ADR-0025 — Retain CP2 and CP3 Blocked Until Named Accountability

## Decision

Retain:

```text
CP0  Authorized and complete
CP1  Authorized and complete
CP2  Blocked and not executed
CP3  Blocked and not executed
```

CP2 requires named staff, verified responsibility, relevant protocol
approvals, bounded evidence controls, and a separate authorization.

CP3 additionally requires affected-person, jurisdiction, professional,
safeguarding, accessibility, and independent facilitation readiness.

## Boundary

A complete accountability framework is not named accountability.

Tabletop success is not human-review activation.

## Day 18 confirmation

Conditional pre-authorization does not satisfy named accountability.

CP2 and CP3 remain Blocked.
