# ADR-0015 — Retain RFC-0001 Through RFC-0003 at Proposed After Round 2

## Decision

```text
RFC-0001  0.2.0-draft.1  Proposed
RFC-0002  0.2.0-draft.1  Proposed
RFC-0003  0.2.0-draft.1  Proposed
```

## Reasons

- four ambiguity questions require additional source or reference profiles;
- the machine requirement and implementation test registries require
  re-baselining for the expanded drafts;
- affected-person and domain review is incomplete;
- legal and jurisdiction review is incomplete;
- external independent review is incomplete;
- product implementation conformance is not tested;
- all dissent remains open.

Eight ambiguity answers are accepted for internal architecture, but internal
acceptance is not Candidate status.
