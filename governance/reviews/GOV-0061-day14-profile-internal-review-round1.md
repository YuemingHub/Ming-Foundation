## Principal risks

1. Participation levels may be misused as a score of maturity, obedience,
   intelligence, or worth.
2. Representative-evidence levels may encourage unnecessary document
   collection or convert care into authority.
3. Retention review intervals may be misread as permission to retain.
4. Service and resource classifications may create false reassurance without
   current staffing, performance, local, accessibility, and safety evidence.
