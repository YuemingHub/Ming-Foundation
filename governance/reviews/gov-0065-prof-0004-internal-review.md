## Required revisions

- Make freshness intervals risk-, location-, and context-sensitive.
- Define evidence required for active-handoff claims.
- Add accessibility, language, age range, eligibility, cost, and safe-contact metadata.
- Require independent or local verification for high-risk resources.

## Decision

The profile remains Proposed.

A completed internal review does not replace affected-person, domain,
jurisdiction, or implementation evidence.
