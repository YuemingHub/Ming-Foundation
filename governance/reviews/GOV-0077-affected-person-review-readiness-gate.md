## Passed gates

1. Profile source revision;
2. Profile source tests;
3. Profile Internal Review Round 2;
4. affected-person review instruments;
5. safeguards and prohibited-collection boundary.

## Blocked gates

1. responsible role assignment;
2. protocol approvals;
3. restricted evidence environment;
4. jurisdiction and professional qualification.

## Transition rule

The state may become `ReadyToSchedule` only after:

- named accountable people accept all required roles;
- conflict and qualification checks are completed;
- all required protocols are approved;
- restricted evidence storage, access, separation, retention, deletion, and
  incident controls are provisioned and tested;
- the jurisdiction and professional-duty route is approved;
- an independent facilitator is assigned where required;
- a separate activation decision is recorded.

No recruitment or session may begin under the Day 15 state.
