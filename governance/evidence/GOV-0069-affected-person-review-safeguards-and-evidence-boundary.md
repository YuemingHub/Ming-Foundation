# GOV-0069 — Affected-Person Review Safeguards and Evidence Boundary

## Required safeguards

- voluntary participation and comprehensible information;
- appropriate consent and assent;
- no service, grade, treatment, payment, or access penalty for refusal;
- right to skip, stop, withdraw, and request deletion;
- minimum demographic data;
- pseudonymous evidence by default;
- separated contact and evidence records;
- restricted access and retention;
- accessibility and language support;
- distress and safeguarding stop route;
- no automated training or personalization from review evidence;
- preservation of disagreement, counterexamples, and minority views.

## Prohibited collection

General review instruments must not collect:

- identifiable child or family case narratives;
- current crisis reports;
- medical, psychiatric, legal, immigration, financial, or school records;
- passwords, credentials, private messages, or device contents;
- third-party evidence without an approved basis.
