# GOV-0106 — Restricted Nomination Intake and Verification Protocol

## Required sequence

```text
Private nomination
→ voluntary consent to assessment
→ restricted identity record
→ qualification review
→ conflict review
→ independence review
→ role acceptance
→ scope and expiry
→ recusal and replacement
→ active assignment decision
```

## Rules

- declining a nomination creates no penalty;
- friendship, founder authority, professional title, or prior collaboration
  is not qualification evidence;
- the same person cannot approve their own conflict or qualification result;
- a slot remains open while any required evidence is missing;
- public status may change only after the corresponding restricted decision
  exists;
- the public repository records references and aggregate states, not private
  details.

## Current result

No nomination or verification was executed by Day 18.
