# GOV-0095 — Small-Team Accountability and Separation of Duties

## Minimum topology

```text
CP2 internal staff rehearsal:
minimum 3 distinct people

CP3 affected-person pilot:
minimum 4 distinct people
```

CP3 requires separately qualified independent facilitation or
jurisdiction/professional review beyond a minimum three-person operating
core.

## Conditional role clusters

A small team may combine:

1. program and recruitment coordination;
2. safeguarding and facilitation;
3. evidence, privacy, and accessibility;
4. separately qualified jurisdiction and professional review.

Combination is allowed only where the machine-readable profile's conditions
are met.

## Absolute separation

A person MUST NOT be the sole reviewer or closer when they:

- are the subject or possible source of harm;
- recruited or directly supervise a dependent participant;
- caused the evidence-access, export, deletion, or privacy incident;
- are the disputed representative;
- lack the required jurisdiction or professional competence;
- are asked to act as both program owner and sole independent facilitator for
  an affected-person pilot.
