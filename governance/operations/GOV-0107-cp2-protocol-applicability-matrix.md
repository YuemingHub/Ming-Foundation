# GOV-0107 — CP2 Protocol Applicability Matrix

## Result

```text
Protocols assessed:        7
Required or adapted:       6
Not applicable to CP2:     1
Approved for CP2 human use: 0
```

Required or adapted:

- staff participation and stop rights;
- distress, disclosure, safeguarding, and stop;
- restricted evidence handling;
- accessibility and communication;
- non-coercive participation and compensation;
- jurisdiction and professional-duty qualification.

The child and adolescent independent-facilitation protocol is not applicable
to CP2 because CP2 contains no child, adolescent, family, client, or
affected-person participation.

That protocol remains required for CP3.
