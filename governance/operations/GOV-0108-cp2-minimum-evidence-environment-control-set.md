# GOV-0108 — CP2 Minimum Evidence Environment Control Set

## Required controls

1. Git-ignored restricted workspace;
2. role-based local access;
3. identity/contact separation;
4. retention and deletion schedule;
5. access, stop, and incident logging;
6. export and copy prohibition by default;
7. backup and restoration suppression;
8. withdrawal and deletion verification.

## Permitted scope

The environment may hold restricted staff role, acceptance, access,
incident, and synthetic rehearsal records.

It MUST NOT hold affected-person or real case evidence.
