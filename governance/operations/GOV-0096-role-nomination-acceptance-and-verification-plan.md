# GOV-0096 — Role Nomination Acceptance and Verification Plan

## Activation workflow

```text
DraftNomination
→ ConsentToAssessment
→ RestrictedIdentityRecorded
→ QualificationEvidenceSubmitted
→ ConflictCheckCompleted
→ IndependenceCheckCompleted
→ RoleAccepted
→ ScopeAndExpiryRecorded
→ BackupOrReplacementRecorded
→ Active
```

A later state MUST NOT be inferred from an earlier state.

## Required evidence

Every active assignment requires:

- voluntary acceptance;
- restricted identity and contact record;
- relevant competence and limitations;
- conflict and independence review;
- prohibited authority;
- scope and expiry;
- briefing or training;
- recusal, replacement, absence, and emergency-stop routes.
