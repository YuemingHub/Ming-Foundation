# GOV-0085 — Review Protocol Approval and Human-Use Authorization Standard

## Separation rule

```text
Protocol designed
≠ protocol reviewed for synthetic rehearsal
≠ protocol approved for human use
≠ review session authorized
```

Day 16 grants design approval for CP0 and CP1 synthetic use only.

## Human-use approval record

A human-use approval MUST include:

- accountable role and independent reviewer;
- exact participant group and operating context;
- consent and assent basis;
- safeguarding and stop route;
- accessibility and language support;
- evidence fields and prohibited fields;
- storage, access, retention, deletion, and incident controls;
- compensation and non-coercion review;
- jurisdiction and professional qualification;
- version, expiry, material-change triggers, and revocation.
