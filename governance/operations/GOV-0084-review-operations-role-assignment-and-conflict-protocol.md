# GOV-0084 — Review Operations Role Assignment and Conflict Protocol

## Purpose

Define how the eight Day 15 review-operation roles become real assignments
without treating a role name as evidence that a qualified person exists.

## Assignment gates

Every assignment MUST record:

- named person and contact route in a restricted register;
- role acceptance;
- scope and expiry;
- relevant qualifications and limits;
- conflict, independence, and dual-role assessment;
- training or briefing completion;
- delegation and absence cover;
- recusal, replacement, and emergency stop route;
- decision authority and prohibited authority.

## Conflict rules

A person MUST NOT approve or close a matter when they:

- are the subject of the concern;
- may be the source of harm;
- benefit materially from the result;
- recruited or supervises the participant in a coercive context;
- verified the evidence and is the sole final reviewer;
- cannot protect confidentiality or independence.

## Day 16 state

```text
Roles defined:  8
Roles assigned: 0
Conflict checks completed: 0
Qualification checks completed: 0
Human review authority: none
```

The protocol is Accepted. No person is assigned by this document.
