# GOV-0094 — Named Accountability and Public Identity Boundary

## Decision boundary

Human review requires real, named accountability.

The public repository does not require publication of real names, personal
contact details, identity documents, certificates, signatures, or conflict
evidence.

The public layer records:

- public accountability ID;
- role and responsibility;
- nomination, acceptance, check, and activation states;
- restricted evidence references;
- scope, expiry, recusal, and replacement status.

The restricted layer stores the identifying and verification evidence
outside the public repository.

## Prohibition

A role title, candidate category, AI-generated name, organizational title, or
private recollection MUST NOT be recorded as a real assignment.

No person is assigned by Day 17.
